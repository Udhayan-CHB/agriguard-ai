Wheat is a major cereal crop grown in temperate regions.

Common problems and solutions:
- Yellowing: Nitrogen deficiency is the primary cause. Top dress with urea (50 kg/ha) at tillering stage.
- Rust: Wheat stem rust (Puccinia graminis) causes reddish-brown pustules. Apply propiconazole and use resistant cultivars.
- Powdery mildew: White powdery patches on leaves. Use sulfur-based fungicides or neem oil.
- Sustainable practice: Zero tillage reduces soil erosion and retains moisture.
- Market: Wheat prices are influenced by global supply; average price is $0.30/kg.
